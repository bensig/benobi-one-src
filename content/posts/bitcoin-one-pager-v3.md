## The Difficulty Adjustment: The Key to Understanding Bitcoin's Value

One of the most important additions to version 3 is an explanation of the difficulty adjustment and how mining works. I read somewhere that there's no crossover between people who can't explain the difficulty adjustment and people who don't see value in Bitcoin.

In other words: if someone doesn't see value in Bitcoin, they usually cannot explain how the difficulty adjustment works.

This really makes sense - it's not the simplest concept, but it's also not that hard to understand. It's absolutely fundamental to why Bitcoin works. So I wanted to attempt making this concept accessible in the Bitcoin one pager.
