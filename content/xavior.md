## The Problem

The average human lifespan is about 20,000 days. A lot of those days are spent switching between apps, chasing notifications, and trying to keep track of everything.

You have a CRM for contacts. A calendar for events. A project manager for tasks. A notes app for ideas. A docs tool for writing. Each one sends alerts. Each one has its own search. None of them talk to each other. The result is that the tools meant to help you stay organized are actually the reason you can't focus.

We're drowning in productivity software and getting less done.

</div>

<div class="mp-section">

## What XAVIOR Does

XAVIOR brings everything into a single AI-powered command center — CRM, projects, tasks, calendar, docs, and notes in one workspace.

Unlike generic AI assistants, XAVIOR runs on 10 custom fine-tuned models, each specialized for a different domain: contacts, events, tasks, search, writing, and more. The AI understands context, not just data — surfacing what matters, what's stuck, and what to do next.

Instead of checking six apps every morning, you talk to one AI that already knows your world.
